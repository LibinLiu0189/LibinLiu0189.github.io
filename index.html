Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor from Shandong Computer Science Center, Qilu University of Technology (Shandong Academy of Sciences). From 2020 to 2021, I was with Tencent and Huawei Hong Kong Research Center. I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong in 2020, advised by Dr. Hong Xu. Before joining CityU in 2016, I got my B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University.

Research Interests

I work on systems and networking. Currently, I am interested in the area of building intelligent networking systems, especially accelerating data tranmission and operation and management for large-scale network devices. Welcome to discuss with me!
