Biography

I am currently an Assistant Researcher at Beijing Zhongguancun Laboratory. From 2020 to 2021, I was with Tencent and Theory Laboratory, Huawei Hong Kong Research Center. I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong in 2020, advised by Dr. Hong Xu. Before joining CityU, I got my B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University.

Research Interests

I work on routing security, network operation and management, and machine learning systems. Currently, I am interested in the area of building intelligent and secure networking systems, especially easing the operation and management for large-scale network.
